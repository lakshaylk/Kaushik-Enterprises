We have great pleasure in introducing that we deals in PNEUMATIC PRESS SPARE PARTS

We are supplying clutch and brake liners (friction segment) to the users of Kaushico, Ameteep, Emco Pressmaster, Narendra, Godrej, HMT, ISGEC & SEW presses. Beside we are also supplying specific sizes for specific requirement for imported machines like Erfurt, forging industry & sheet metal industry. We are also supplying blocks commonly known as Gutka upto 60mm of thickness to various customers in forging indutry & sheet metal industry. These liners are not only used in the presses but also used in cranes, fork lifts & electric motors.
